Welcome to GK-Nexus, the comprehensive business management platform for Green Crescent Management Consultancy (GCMC) and Kareem Abdul-Jabar Tax & Accounting Services (KAJ).

## What is GK-Nexus?

GK-Nexus is a modern business management platform designed specifically for professional service firms in Guyana. It streamlines client management, matter tracking, document management, and service delivery for both GCMC and KAJ.

## Two Businesses, One Platform

### Green Crescent Management Consultancy (GCMC)

GCMC provides:
- **Training Programs** - HR, Customer Relations, Co-operatives, Organizational Management
- **Business Development** - Company Incorporation, Business Registration
- **Paralegal Services** - Affidavits, Agreements, Wills, Contracts
- **Immigration Services** - Work Permits, Citizenship, Business Visas
- **Business Proposals** - Land Occupation, Investment, Start-up Proposals

### Kareem Abdul-Jabar Tax & Accounting Services (KAJ)

KAJ provides:
- **Income Tax Returns** - Individual, Corporate, Self-Employed
- **Compliance Services** - Tender, Work Permit, Land Transfer, Firearm
- **PAYE Returns** - Monthly and Annual Employer Payroll Tax
- **Financial Statements** - Loan Applications, Permits, Projections
- **NIS Services** - Registration, Contributions, Pension Applications

## Getting Started as a Staff Member

### 1. Login

Navigate to the GK-Nexus platform and login with your credentials.

### 2. Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard shows:
- Recent activity
- Upcoming deadlines
- Quick action buttons
- Statistics and metrics

### 3. Creating a Client

1. Click "Clients" in the navigation
2. Click "Add Client" button
3. Enter client information:
   - Full name
   - Email address
   - Phone number
   - Address
   - Client type (INDIVIDUAL or ORGANIZATION)
   - Business (GCMC or KAJ)
4. Click "Save"

### 4. Creating a Matter

1. Navigate to the client's profile
2. Click "Create Matter" or go to Matters section
3. Enter matter details:
   - Matter title
   - Service type (select from dropdown)
   - Status (NEW by default)
   - Assigned staff member
   - Description
   - Deadline (if applicable)
4. Click "Create Matter"

### 5. Uploading Documents

1. Open the matter
2. Click "Upload Document"
3. Select file from your computer
4. Choose document category
5. Add description (optional)
6. Click "Upload"

### 6. Managing Deadlines

1. Click "Deadlines" in navigation
2. View calendar of upcoming deadlines
3. Filter by staff or matter
4. Click deadline to view details
5. Mark as complete when done

## Getting Started as a Client

### 1. Accessing Your Portal

You'll receive an email invitation to access the client portal with:
- Your unique portal link
- Login credentials
- Instructions

### 2. Portal Dashboard

Your portal shows:
- Your active matters
- Document access
- Important updates
- Contact information

### 3. Viewing Your Matters

1. Click on any matter to view details
2. See current status and progress
3. View assigned staff member
4. Check upcoming deadlines

### 4. Uploading Documents

When staff request documents:
1. Click "Upload Document" on the matter
2. Select your file
3. Add a description
4. Click "Upload"
5. Staff will be notified

### 5. Downloading Documents

To download completed documents:
1. Navigate to your matter
2. Find the document in the list
3. Click the download button
4. Save to your computer

## Common Workflows

### GCMC: Company Incorporation

1. **Client Consultation** - Create client and initial matter
2. **Document Collection** - Request documents via portal
3. **Name Search** - Update matter status to IN_PROGRESS
4. **Document Preparation** - Upload drafts for review
5. **Submission** - Update status to PENDING
6. **Collection** - Upload final certificate
7. **Completion** - Mark matter as COMPLETED

### KAJ: Individual Tax Return

1. **Client Setup** - Create client profile
2. **Matter Creation** - Create tax return matter
3. **Document Request** - Request income and expense documents
4. **Preparation** - Status to IN_PROGRESS
5. **Review** - Client reviews draft return
6. **Filing** - Submit to GRA
7. **Completion** - Upload filed return, mark COMPLETED
