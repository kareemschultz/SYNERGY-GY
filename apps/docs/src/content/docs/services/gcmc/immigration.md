GCMC provides complete immigration application services to assist foreign nationals with work permits, citizenship applications, and business visas for Guyana.

## Work Permits

Complete application service for obtaining work authorization for foreign nationals employed in Guyana.

### Service Types

- **New Work Permit Applications** - First-time work authorization
- **Work Permit Renewals** - Extension of existing permits
- **Work Permit Amendments** - Employer or position changes
- **Dependent Permits** - Family members of work permit holders

### Timeline

- **New Applications:** 4-8 weeks
- **Renewals:** 3-6 weeks
- **Urgent Processing:** 2-3 weeks (additional fees apply)

### Pricing

- New Work Permit: $50,000 - $100,000
- Renewal: $40,000 - $80,000
- Amendment: $35,000 - $60,000
- Dependent Permit: $30,000 - $50,000
- Urgent Processing: +50% premium

### Document Requirements

**For Applicant:**
- Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity)
- Passport-size photographs (4)
- Birth certificate
- Police clearance certificate (from country of origin)
- Medical certificate (approved physician)
- Educational qualifications/certificates
- Professional certifications/licenses
- Resume/CV
- Proof of accommodation in Guyana
- Marriage certificate (if applicable)

**For Employer:**
- Company registration/incorporation certificate
- TIN certificate
- NIS compliance certificate
- Business license
- Employment contract/letter of offer
- Job description
- Justification letter (why foreign worker needed)
- Advertisement proof (local recruitment efforts)
- Company profile

### Workflow

1. **Initial Consultation (Week 1)** - Assess eligibility and provide document checklist
2. **Document Collection (Weeks 1-2)** - Gather all required documents
3. **Application Preparation (Week 3)** - Complete forms and organize package
4. **Submission (Week 4)** - Submit to Ministry of Home Affairs
5. **Processing and Follow-up (Weeks 5-7)** - Monitor status and respond to queries
6. **Collection and Delivery (Week 8)** - Collect and deliver approved permit

### Government Agencies Involved

- Ministry of Home Affairs (Citizenship and Immigration)
- Ministry of Labour (work permit verification)
- Ministry of Health (medical clearance)
- Guyana Police Force (local clearance if applicable)

### Common Grounds for Approval

- Specialized skills not available locally
- Management positions
- Technical expertise
- Investment-related employment
- Regional/international organization positions

## Citizenship

Application assistance for Guyana citizenship through naturalization, registration, or descent.

### Service Types

- **Citizenship by Naturalization** - Residence-based (7+ years)
- **Citizenship by Registration** - Marriage or descent
- **Citizenship by Investment** - Certain investment cases
- **Dual Citizenship Confirmation** - Dual citizenship verification

### Timeline

**6-18 months** (government processing time varies)

### Pricing

- Naturalization: $120,000 - $200,000
- Registration: $80,000 - $150,000
- Consultation: $20,000 - $30,000

### Document Requirements

**For Naturalization (7+ years residence):**
- Valid passport
- Birth certificate
- Police clearance (Guyana and country of origin)
- Proof of continuous residence (7 years minimum)
- Residential address proof
- Employment history/proof of income
- Tax compliance certificate (GRA)
- NIS contributions record
- Character references (2-3)
- Marriage certificate (if applicable)
- Passport photographs (6)
- Renunciation of previous citizenship (if required)

**For Registration (by marriage or descent):**
- Birth certificate
- Marriage certificate (if applicable)
- Spouse's Guyana citizenship proof
- Parent's Guyana citizenship proof (if by descent)
- Police clearance
- Passport photographs
- Proof of Guyanese parent/grandparent

### Citizenship Requirements

- Age 18 or older
- 7 years continuous ordinary residence (naturalization)
- Good character
- Adequate knowledge of English
- Intention to reside in Guyana
- Oath of allegiance

### Government Agencies Involved

- Ministry of Home Affairs (Citizenship Division)
- Guyana Police Force (background checks)
- Guyana Revenue Authority (tax clearance)
- National Insurance Scheme (contribution verification)

### Post-Citizenship Services

- Guyana passport application
- National ID application
- Updating of personal records

## Business Visa

Application support for business visas enabling foreign nationals to visit Guyana for business activities, meetings, conferences, or exploratory visits.

### Service Types

- Single entry business visa
- Multiple entry business visa
- Conference/event visas
- Investment exploration visas
- Business mission visas

### Timeline

**1-3 weeks**

### Pricing

- Single Entry: $25,000 - $40,000
- Multiple Entry: $35,000 - $60,000
- Urgent Processing: +$15,000

### Document Requirements

- Valid passport (6+ months validity)
- Completed visa application form
- Passport photographs (2)
- Letter of invitation from Guyana business/organization
- Company registration (inviting company)
- Applicant's company letter/business registration
- Proof of business relationship
- Travel itinerary
- Hotel reservation or accommodation proof
- Financial proof (bank statements)
- Return ticket or travel plans
- Travel insurance (recommended)

### Visa Conditions

- Business activities only (no employment)
- Duration typically 30-90 days
- No remuneration from Guyana sources
- Must depart before visa expiry

### Business Activities Permitted

- Attend meetings and conferences
- Negotiate contracts
- Conduct site visits
- Explore investment opportunities
- Attend trade shows
- Business consultations

## Post-Approval Requirements

- Work permit must be stamped in passport
- Employer must report commencement of employment
- Renewal applications should begin 2-3 months before expiry
- Changes in employment must be reported
