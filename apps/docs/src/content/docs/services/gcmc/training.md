Green Crescent Management Consultancy (GCMC) offers comprehensive professional development programs designed to enhance organizational and individual capacity across various sectors in Guyana.

## Available Training Programs

### Human Resource Management Training

Professional development program covering recruitment, employee relations, performance management, labor laws, and HR best practices in Guyana.

**Topics Covered:**
- Recruitment and selection processes
- Employee onboarding and orientation
- Performance management systems
- Disciplinary procedures and grievance handling
- Guyana Labour Act compliance
- Payroll and benefits administration
- HR record-keeping requirements

**Target Audience:** HR professionals, managers, business owners, administrative staff

**Duration:** 2-5 days (customizable)

**Pricing:**
- 2 Days: $30,000 - $50,000 per person
- 5 Days: $50,000 - $80,000 per person
- Corporate packages: Negotiable (10+ participants)

Service excellence training focused on customer engagement, conflict resolution, communication skills, and building long-term customer relationships.

**Topics Covered:**
- Understanding customer needs and expectations
- Effective communication techniques
- Handling difficult customers
- Complaint resolution strategies
- Building customer loyalty
- Service recovery techniques
- Digital customer service

**Target Audience:** Customer service representatives, front-line staff, sales teams, managers

**Duration:** 1-3 days

**Pricing:**
- 1 Day: $15,000 - $25,000 per person
- 2-3 Days: $30,000 - $50,000 per person

### Co-operatives and Credit Unions Training

Specialized training on cooperative principles, governance, financial management, and regulatory compliance for cooperative societies and credit unions.

**Topics Covered:**
- Cooperative principles and values
- Governance and board responsibilities
- Financial management for cooperatives
- Member relations and education
- Guyana Co-operative Societies Act compliance
- Record-keeping and reporting
- Credit union specific regulations
- Risk management

**Target Audience:** Cooperative board members, credit union officials, management committees, treasurers

**Duration:** 2-5 days

**Pricing:**
- 2 Days: $30,000 - $50,000 per person
- 5 Days: $50,000 - $80,000 per person
- Cooperative group rates available

**Government Agencies Involved:**
- Department of Co-operatives (Ministry of Labour)
- Credit Union League

## Training Workflow

1. **Initial Consultation** - Needs assessment and curriculum discussion
2. **Program Registration** - Participant enrollment and payment
3. **Training Delivery** - Interactive sessions with practical exercises
4. **Assessment** - Evaluation of participant learning
5. **Certification** - Certificate of completion issued
6. **Follow-up Support** - Post-training support period (30-60 days)

## Document Requirements

- Registration form
- Proof of payment
- Copy of ID for certificate issuance

## Delivery Formats

- In-house corporate training (at client location)
- Public workshops (at GCMC venue or conference facility)
- One-on-one coaching sessions
- Customized programs for specific industries
- Follow-up support and mentorship available
