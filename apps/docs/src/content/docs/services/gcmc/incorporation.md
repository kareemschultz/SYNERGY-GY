GCMC provides comprehensive incorporation and business registration services, handling all aspects of establishing your legal business entity in Guyana.

## Company Incorporation

Complete service for incorporating a limited liability company in Guyana, including name search, document preparation, registration, and post-incorporation compliance.

### Service Details

- Company name availability search and reservation
- Preparation of Memorandum of Association
- Preparation of Articles of Association
- Director consents and declarations
- Registered office designation
- Share structure and allocation
- Submission to Deeds Registry
- Certificate of Incorporation collection
- Post-incorporation setup (TIN, NIS, bank account guidance)

### Timeline

**Typical Timeline:** 7-14 business days

**Workflow:**
1. **Initial Consultation (Day 1)** - Discuss business structure and requirements
2. **Name Search (Days 1-2)** - Submit name search to Deeds Registry and verify availability
3. **Document Preparation (Days 3-5)** - Draft incorporation documents and obtain client approval
4. **Registration (Days 6-10)** - Submit to Deeds Registry and pay registration fees
5. **Collection (Days 11-12)** - Collect Certificate of Incorporation
6. **Post-Incorporation (Days 13-14)** - Assist with TIN, NIS, and bank account setup

### Pricing

$80,000 - $150,000 (depending on complexity)

### Document Requirements

**Directors:**
- National ID or Passport (minimum 2 directors)
- Proof of address
- TIN (if available)
- Director signatures

**Company Information:**
- Proposed company names (3 options preferred)
- Registered office address proof
- Share structure details
- Business objectives and activities

### Government Agencies Involved

- **Deeds Registry** (Ministry of Legal Affairs) - Company registration
- **Guyana Revenue Authority (GRA)** - TIN registration
- **National Insurance Scheme** - NIS registration
- **Georgetown City Council** - Business license (if applicable)

### Deliverables

- Certificate of Incorporation
- Memorandum of Association (sealed)
- Articles of Association (sealed)
- Company registration number
- Share certificates
- Company formation package
- Compliance calendar

## Business Registration

Registration of business names, sole proprietorships, and partnerships with the Deeds Registry.

### Service Types

- **Business Name Registration** - Sole proprietor registration
- **Partnership Registration** - Two or more partners
- **Business Name Renewal** - Renew existing registration
- **Change of Business Details** - Update registered information

### Timeline

**Typical Timeline:** 3-7 business days

### Pricing

- Business Name: $15,000 - $25,000
- Partnership: $20,000 - $35,000
- Renewals: $12,000 - $20,000

### Document Requirements

**For Sole Proprietor:**
- National ID or Passport
- Proof of address
- TIN (if available)
- Proposed business names (2-3 options)
- Business address proof
- Signature specimen

**For Partnership:**
- All partners' IDs
- Partnership agreement (draft or final)
- Proof of address for all partners
- Business address proof
- Proposed business names
- Capital contribution details

### Workflow

1. **Consultation (Day 1)** - Verify requirements and conduct name search
2. **Preparation (Days 2-3)** - Complete forms and draft agreements
3. **Submission (Day 4)** - Submit to Deeds Registry
4. **Processing (Days 5-6)** - Registry processing
5. **Collection (Day 7)** - Deliver certificate and provide guidance

### Post-Registration Support

- TIN registration assistance
- Business license application guidance
- NIS registration (if hiring employees)
- Bank account opening documentation
- Compliance requirements checklist
