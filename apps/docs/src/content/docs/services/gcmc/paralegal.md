GCMC provides comprehensive paralegal services for individuals and businesses, offering professional legal document preparation and notarization services.

## Affidavits

Preparation and commissioning of sworn written statements used for legal, immigration, or administrative purposes.

### Common Types

- **Affidavit of Support** - Immigration sponsorship
- **Affidavit of Common Law Union** - Relationship proof
- **Affidavit of Single Status** - Marriage status declaration
- **Affidavit of Name Change** - Legal name changes
- **Affidavit of Loss** - Lost documents
- **Affidavit of Ownership** - Property ownership
- **Affidavit of Residency** - Proof of residence
- **General Sworn Statements** - Various purposes

### Timeline & Pricing

- **Timeline:** Same day to 1 business day
- **Pricing:** $5,000 - $15,000 (depending on complexity and urgency)

### Workflow

1. Client consultation and fact gathering
2. Draft affidavit preparation
3. Client review and approval
4. Commissioning before Justice of the Peace or Commissioner of Oaths
5. Delivery of original notarized affidavit

## Wills

Preparation of testamentary documents for the distribution of assets upon death, ensuring legal compliance with Guyana's succession laws.

### Service Includes

- Consultation on estate planning
- Will drafting
- Asset inventory assistance
- Executor designation
- Witness arrangement
- Notarization and safekeeping advice

### Timeline & Pricing

- **Timeline:** 3-7 business days
- **Pricing:** $25,000 - $75,000 (depending on estate complexity)

### Legal Requirements

- Testator must be 18+ years old
- Sound mind and understanding
- Two independent witnesses required
- Cannot be forced or under duress

### Document Requirements

- Valid ID of testator
- List of assets (property, accounts, investments)
- Beneficiary details (names, relationships, IDs)
- Executor designation
- Guardian designation (if minor children)
- Specific bequests details
- Debt information

### Safekeeping Options

- Client retention (safety deposit box)
- Attorney safekeeping
- Registry deposit (Supreme Court)
