## PAYE Calculation Process

### Step 1: Gross Pay Calculation

Calculate total compensation including:
- Basic salary/wages
- Overtime pay
- Bonuses and commissions
- Allowances (taxable)
- Benefits in kind (valued)

### Step 2: PAYE Tax Withholding

Apply current GRA tax tables:
- Personal allowance: $780,000 annually (2025)
- Tax rates on income above threshold
- Progressive tax brackets
- Monthly withholding calculation

### Step 3: NIS Contributions

Calculate statutory contributions:
- Employee contribution: 5.6% of NIS-able earnings
- Employer contribution: 8.4% of NIS-able earnings
- Maximum insurable earnings: $280,000 per month
- Maximum monthly contribution: $39,200 total

### Step 4: Net Pay

Compute employee net pay:
- Gross pay
- Less PAYE withholding
- Less employee NIS contribution
- Less other authorized deductions
- Equals net pay

## PAYE Compliance Requirements

### Employer Obligations

- Register as PAYE employer with GRA
- Obtain TIN for all employees
- Withhold correct PAYE from each pay period
- File monthly PAYE returns by 15th of following month
- Remit withheld taxes to GRA
- Issue employee tax certificates annually
- Maintain payroll records for 7 years

### Penalties for Non-Compliance

- Late filing: $5,000 per day
- Late payment: Interest on unpaid taxes
- Incorrect returns: Penalties and interest
- Failure to issue certificates: $5,000 per employee

## Employee Tax Certificates

Issued to each employee showing:
- Employee identification (name, TIN, NIS)
- Total gross wages for the year
- Total PAYE withheld
- Total NIS contributions (employee portion)
- Employer details
- Tax year

**Distribution Deadline:** Within 30 days of year-end (by January 31)
