KAJ prepares professional income and expenditure statements for various applications including bank loans, firearm licenses, investments, and business planning.

## Bank Loan Statements

Comprehensive financial statements for loan applications, credit facilities, and mortgages.

### Service Includes

- Income analysis from all sources
- Expenditure breakdown by category
- Cash flow projections
- Debt service capacity analysis
- Net worth statement
- Financial ratios
- Professional accountant certification

### Timeline

**3-5 business days**

### Pricing

$30,000 - $60,000

### Required Documents

- Bank statements (6-12 months, all accounts)
- Employment income (pay slips, contracts)
- Business income (if self-employed)
- Investment income documentation
- Existing loan statements
- Asset valuations
- Monthly expense receipts/records

### Statement Components

**Income Section:**
- Employment income (gross and net)
- Business/self-employment income
- Investment income (dividends, interest)
- Rental income
- Other recurring income sources

**Expenditure Section:**
- Housing (rent/mortgage, utilities, maintenance)
- Transportation (vehicle payments, fuel, insurance)
- Food and household expenses
- Insurance premiums
- Loan payments and debt service
- Education expenses
- Medical expenses
- Personal and discretionary spending

**Analysis:**
- Total monthly income
- Total monthly expenditure
- Net disposable income
- Debt-to-income ratio
- Loan affordability assessment

## Statement Preparation Workflow

### For Individual Applications

1. **Initial Consultation** - Determine statement purpose and requirements
2. **Document Collection** - Gather all financial documentation
3. **Income Analysis** - Analyze and verify all income sources
4. **Expenditure Analysis** - Detail and categorize monthly expenses
5. **Net Worth Calculation** - List assets and liabilities
6. **Statement Preparation** - Prepare comprehensive professional statement
7. **Review and Certification** - Professional accountant review and sign-off
8. **Delivery** - Provide certified copies to client

### For Business Applications

1. **Historical Review** - Analyze past financial performance
2. **Revenue Analysis** - Review trends and project future income
3. **Expense Analysis** - Detail operating costs and projections
4. **Cash Flow Modeling** - Create monthly projections
5. **Scenario Analysis** - Model different outcomes
6. **Professional Presentation** - Format and certify projections
7. **Client Review** - Present and explain projections
8. **Finalization** - Deliver final certified documents
