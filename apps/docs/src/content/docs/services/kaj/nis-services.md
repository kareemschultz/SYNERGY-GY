KAJ provides complete National Insurance Scheme (NIS) services including employer and employee registration, monthly contributions, compliance management, and pension applications.

## Employer Registration

Register your business as an NIS employer to comply with social security requirements.

### Service Details

- Complete NIS Form E1 (Employer Registration)
- Submit to NIS headquarters or regional office
- Follow up on application status
- Obtain NIS employer number
- Provide compliance guidelines
- Set up monthly contribution schedule

### Timeline

**3-5 business days**

### Pricing

$10,000 - $15,000

### Required Documents

- Business registration or incorporation certificate
- TIN certificate
- Business address and contact information
- Ownership/director information

### Employer Obligations

Once registered, employers must:
- Register all employees with NIS
- Deduct employee contributions from wages (5.6%)
- Pay employer contributions (8.4%)
- Submit monthly schedules by 14th of following month
- Maintain accurate payroll records
- Report employee changes and terminations

## Employee Registration

Register employees with NIS to ensure social security coverage.

### Service Details

- Complete NIS Form E2 for each employee
- Submit batch registrations to NIS
- Follow up on NIS number issuance
- Update employer records with NIS numbers
- File employee documentation

### Timeline

**2-3 business days**

### Pricing

$3,000 - $5,000 per employee

### Required Documents

- Date of birth
- Birth certificate or national ID
- TIN
- Address and contact information

## Monthly Schedules of Contributions

Prepare and submit monthly NIS contribution schedules for all employees.

### Service Details

- Calculate NIS-able earnings for each employee
- Compute employee contributions (5.6%)
- Compute employer contributions (8.4%)
- Prepare Schedule of Contributions
- Submit to NIS by 14th of following month
- Prepare payment voucher
- Advise client of total amount due

### Timeline

**2-3 business days** (completed before 14th deadline)

### Pricing

$8,000 - $20,000 per month

### NIS-able Earnings Include

- Basic salary
- Overtime pay
- Bonuses
- Commissions
- Certain allowances

### Current NIS Rates (2025)

- **Employee Contribution:** 5.6% of NIS-able earnings
- **Employer Contribution:** 8.4% of NIS-able earnings
- **Total:** 14.0% of NIS-able earnings
- **Maximum Insurable Earnings:** $280,000 per month
- **Maximum Monthly Contribution:** $39,200 total ($15,680 employee + $23,520 employer)

### Filing Deadline

**14th of following month**

### Penalties

Late payment results in interest and penalties as per NIS Act.

## Pension Queries & Applications

Assist with NIS pension applications, calculate entitlements, and resolve pension issues.

### Types of NIS Pensions

1. **Old Age Pension** - Age 60+ with minimum 750 contributions
2. **Survivors Pension** - For dependents of deceased insured persons
3. **Invalidity Pension** - For permanently disabled insured persons

### Service Details

- Determine pension eligibility
- Review contribution history
- Calculate potential pension amount
- Request NIS contribution statement
- Complete pension application forms
- Submit application with supporting documents
- Follow up on application status
- Verify pension calculation
- Set up direct deposit

### Timeline

**5-10 business days** (plus NIS processing time)

### Pricing

$20,000 - $50,000

### Required Documents

**For Old Age Pension:**
- Birth certificate
- National ID or passport
- Employment history
- Bank account information
- NIS contribution statement

**For Survivors Pension:**
- Death certificate of insured person
- Birth certificates (applicant and dependents)
- Marriage certificate (if applicable)
- Relationship proof
- Bank account information

**For Invalidity Pension:**
- Medical certificates and reports
- Doctor's assessment
- Employment history
- NIS contribution statement
- Birth certificate and ID

### Pension Application Workflow

1. **Initial Consultation** - Determine eligibility and review history
2. **Contribution Verification** - Request and verify NIS records
3. **Document Collection** - Gather all required documents
4. **Application Preparation** - Complete all forms accurately
5. **Submission** - Submit to NIS with supporting documents
6. **Follow-up** - Monitor application status
7. **Verification** - Verify pension calculation upon approval
8. **Setup** - Arrange direct deposit
9. **Client Support** - Provide pension schedule and entitlements

## Common NIS Questions

**When do I need to register as an employer?**
Within 7 days of hiring your first employee.

**What if I miss the contribution deadline?**
Penalties and interest apply. Contact KAJ immediately to resolve.

**Can employees opt out of NIS?**
No, NIS is mandatory for all employees.

**What earnings are NIS-able?**
Basic salary, overtime, bonuses, commissions, and certain allowances up to the maximum of $280,000 monthly.

**How do I check my NIS contribution history?**
KAJ can request an official NIS statement on your behalf.

**When can I apply for my pension?**
At age 60 with minimum 750 contributions. Contact KAJ to verify eligibility.
