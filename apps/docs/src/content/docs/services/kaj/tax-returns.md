Kareem Abdul-Jabar Tax & Accounting Services (KAJ) provides comprehensive income tax return preparation and filing services for all taxpayer categories in Guyana.

## Individual Income Tax Returns

Personal income tax returns for employed and self-employed individuals.

### Service Details

Complete preparation of annual income tax returns including:
- Calculation of gross income from all sources
- Application of allowable deductions and allowances
- Computation of taxable income
- Calculation of tax liability
- Application of tax credits
- Determination of refund or balance due

### Timeline

**3-5 business days** (from receipt of all documents)

### Pricing

$15,000 - $60,000 (depending on complexity)

### Required Documents

**Employed Individuals:**
- Employment income certificates (GRA forms from employer)
- Pay slips (January to December)
- NIS contribution records
- Medical expense receipts (if claiming)
- Mortgage interest statements (if claiming)
- Education expense receipts (if claiming)
- Charitable donation receipts (if claiming)

**Self-Employed/Business Income:**
- Revenue records (sales invoices, receipts)
- Business expense receipts and invoices
- Business bank statements
- Vehicle logs (if claiming vehicle expenses)
- Inventory records (opening and closing)
- Prior year tax returns

### Key Deadlines

- **Filing Deadline:** April 30 (for prior calendar year)
- **Payment Deadline:** April 30 or upon filing
- **Late Filing Penalty:** $10,000 + $1,000/month

### GRA Forms Used

- IT-01 (Income Tax Return)
- IT-03 (Self-Assessment)
- Business schedules (for self-employed)

## Corporate Income Tax Returns

Business income tax filing for companies and partnerships.

### Service Details

- Preparation/review of financial statements
- Tax adjustments for accounting profit
- Calculation of depreciation and capital allowances
- Application of loss carry-forwards
- Computation of chargeable income
- Calculation of corporation tax payable
- Preparation of supporting schedules

### Timeline

**5-10 business days** (depending on complexity)

### Pricing

$50,000 - $300,000 (based on company size and complexity)

### Required Documents

- Audited or unaudited financial statements
- Trial balance
- General ledger
- Fixed asset register with depreciation schedule
- Stock/inventory records (opening and closing)
- Debtors and creditors listings
- Bank statements (all company accounts)
- Payroll summary
- Director remuneration details
- Related party transaction documentation
- Prior year tax returns and computations
- Loss relief documentation (if applicable)

### Key Deadlines

- **Filing Deadline:** 4 months after company year-end
- **Payment Deadline:** With filing
- **Late Filing Penalty:** $10,000 + interest on tax due

### GRA Forms Used

- CT-01 (Corporation Tax Return)
- Supporting schedules as required

## Self-Employed Tax Returns

Tax returns for sole proprietors and independent contractors.

- Business expense categorization
- Home office expense allocation
- Vehicle expense calculations
- Inventory valuation
- Depreciation calculations

### Timeline

### Pricing

## Tax Return Workflow

1. **Initial Consultation** - Review income sources and identify deductions
2. **Document Collection** - Gather all required documentation
3. **Tax Return Preparation** - Calculate income, deductions, and tax liability
4. **Client Review & Approval** - Present return summary and obtain signature
5. **Filing & Submission** - Submit to GRA electronically or by paper
6. **Follow-up** - Track refund status and respond to GRA queries

## Common Deductions and Allowances

**Personal Allowances:**
- Personal income tax threshold: $780,000 annually (2025)
- Medical expenses (with receipts)
- Mortgage interest on primary residence
- Education expenses for children
- Charitable donations to registered charities
- NIS contributions

**Business Deductions:**
- Ordinary and necessary business expenses
- Depreciation on business assets
- Business use of home (proportionate)
- Professional fees and memberships
- Business travel and accommodation
- Employee wages and benefits
- Business insurance premiums
