KAJ provides specialized compliance services to ensure individuals and businesses meet all tax and financial documentation requirements for various regulatory purposes.

## Tender Compliance

Tax compliance documentation for government and private sector tender applications.

### Service Includes

- Review of tax filing status
- Preparation of outstanding returns (if any)
- Certificate of Compliance application
- Tax Clearance Certificate
- NIS compliance verification
- Financial statement preparation (if required)

### Timeline

**5-10 business days**

### Pricing

$25,000 - $50,000

### Required Documents

- Recent tax returns (3 years)
- Proof of tax payments
- NIS compliance certificate
- Financial statements (if required)
- Company registration documents

### Deliverables

- Certificate of Compliance from GRA
- Tax Clearance Certificate
- NIS Compliance Certificate
- Supporting financial documentation

## Land Transfer Compliance

### Service Includes

- Property transfer tax calculations
- Capital gains tax computation (if applicable)
- Certificate of Compliance application
- Tax clearance for property registration
- Financial documentation for buyers/sellers

### Timeline

**7-10 business days**

### Pricing

$30,000 - $60,000

### Required Documents

- Property sale agreement
- Property deed/title
- Prior tax returns (buyer and seller)
- Purchase price documentation
- Property valuation (if available)
- Capital gains calculation basis

### Government Agencies

- Guyana Revenue Authority
- Deeds Registry
- City/Town Council (property tax)

## Compliance Workflow

1. **Consultation** - Understand specific compliance requirement
2. **Status Review** - Review existing tax and NIS status
3. **Document Collection** - Gather required documentation
4. **Preparation** - Prepare financial statements if needed
5. **Filing** - Complete any outstanding returns or payments
6. **Application** - Apply for required certificates from GRA/NIS
7. **Follow-up** - Monitor application status with authorities
8. **Delivery** - Deliver compliance package to client

## Important Compliance Notes

- Most compliance services require being up-to-date with all tax obligations
- Outstanding returns must be filed before certificates can be issued
- All tax payments must be current
- Processing time includes government agency processing (5-15 business days typically)
- Expedited service available for urgent matters (additional fees apply)
