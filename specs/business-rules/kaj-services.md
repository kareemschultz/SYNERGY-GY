## Service Workflows

### Individual Income Tax Filing

```
1. Initial Consultation
   └── Review income sources
   └── Identify deductions/allowances
   └── Assess tax residency status

2. Document Collection
   └── Employment income (GRA forms, pay slips)
   └── Self-employment records
   └── Investment income statements
   └── Rental income records
   └── Charitable donation receipts
   └── Medical expenses
   └── Education expenses
   └── Mortgage interest statements
   └── NIS contribution records

3. Tax Return Preparation
   └── Calculate gross income
   └── Apply allowable deductions
   └── Compute taxable income
   └── Calculate tax liability
   └── Apply tax credits
   └── Determine refund/balance due
   └── Quality review

4. Client Review & Approval
   └── Present tax return summary
   └── Explain calculations
   └── Review supporting documents
   └── Obtain client signature

5. Filing & Submission
   └── Submit to GRA (electronic/paper)
   └── Pay tax due (if applicable)
   └── Obtain acknowledgment receipt
   └── Provide copy to client

6. Follow-up
   └── Track refund status
   └── Respond to GRA queries
   └── File amendments if needed
   └── Archive records (7 years)
```

```

   └── Review all sales (output VAT)

   └── Verify VAT rates applied

   └── Total output VAT collected

   └── Calculate net VAT position
   └── Check non-deductible input VAT
   └── Verify reverse charge VAT

3. Return Preparation
   └── Complete VAT return form
   └── Attach supporting schedules
   └── Reconcile to accounting records
   └── Quality check all figures

4. Client Approval
   └── Present VAT return
   └── Explain calculation
   └── Address questions
   └── Obtain authorization

5. Filing & Payment
   └── Submit to GRA (TRIPS system)

   └── File VAT return copy

   └── Track refund status
```

## Risk Management

### Tax Risk Assessment

**High-Risk Indicators:**
- Large cash transactions
- Significant related party transactions
- Frequent changes in accounting methods
- Aggressive tax positions
- History of GRA audits or disputes
- Incomplete records
- Industry-specific risks (cash businesses, construction, etc.)

**Mitigation Strategies:**
- Enhanced documentation requirements
- Senior-level review mandatory
- Conservative tax positions
- Written explanations of significant items
- Regular compliance reviews
- Client education on tax obligations

### Professional Standards

**Independence:**
- Cannot audit own bookkeeping work
- Separate tax and audit functions
- Disclose conflicts of interest
- Decline engagements with independence issues

**Competence:**
- Stay current with tax law changes
- Attend professional development courses
- Subscribe to GRA updates
- Maintain professional certifications

**Confidentiality:**
- Secure client files (physical and digital)
- Password-protect sensitive documents
- Limit staff access to need-to-know basis
- Shred confidential documents
- Secure email communications
