## Service Workflows

### Work Permit Application

```
1. Initial Consultation
   └── Assess eligibility
   └── Gather requirements list

2. Document Collection
   └── Passport copies
   └── Employment letter
   └── Police clearance
   └── Medical certificate
   └── Qualifications

3. Application Preparation
   └── Complete forms
   └── Organize documents
   └── Quality check

4. Submission
   └── Submit to Ministry of Home Affairs
   └── Pay fees
   └── Receive acknowledgment

5. Follow-up
   └── Track status
   └── Respond to queries
   └── Additional documents if needed

6. Completion
   └── Collect permit
   └── Deliver to client
   └── Record expiration for renewal
```

### Company Incorporation

```
1. Name Search
   └── Check availability
   └── Reserve name

2. Document Preparation
   └── Memorandum of Association
   └── Articles of Association
   └── Director consents
   └── Registered office details

3. Submission
   └── Submit to Deeds Registry
   └── Pay registration fees

4. Certificate Issuance
   └── Collect Certificate of Incorporation
   └── Obtain company number

5. Post-Incorporation
   └── TIN registration
   └── NIS registration
   └── Bank account opening
```

## Document Requirements

### Common Documents

**Individuals:**
- National ID / Passport
- Birth Certificate
- Police Clearance
- Proof of Address
- Photographs

**Businesses:**
- Certificate of Incorporation
- Business Registration
- TIN Certificate
- Director ID documents
- Registered Address Proof

### Service-Specific Requirements

**Work Permit:**
- Valid passport (6+ months)
- Employment contract
- Company letter
- Qualifications/certifications
- Medical certificate
- Police clearance
- Passport photos (4)
- Proof of accommodation

**Company Incorporation:**
- Director IDs (min 2)
- Proposed company name (3 options)
- Registered office address
- Share structure
- Business objectives
