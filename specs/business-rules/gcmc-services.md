**Document Requirements:**

**For Applicant:**

- Passport-size photographs (4)
- Birth certificate
- Police clearance certificate (from country of origin)
- Medical certificate (approved physician)
- Educational qualifications/certificates
- Professional certifications/licenses
- Resume/CV
- Proof of accommodation in Guyana
- Marriage certificate (if applicable)

**For Employer:**
- Company registration/incorporation certificate
- TIN certificate
- NIS compliance certificate
- Business license
- Employment contract/letter of offer
- Job description
- Justification letter (why foreign worker needed)
- Advertisement proof (local recruitment efforts)
- Company profile

**Workflow:**
```
1. Initial Consultation (Week 1)
   └── Assess eligibility
   └── Explain requirements and process
   └── Provide document checklist
   └── Quote provision

2. Document Collection (Weeks 1-2)
   └── Gather applicant documents
   └── Obtain employer documents
   └── Verify authenticity
   └── Arrange medical exam
   └── Obtain police clearance

3. Application Preparation (Week 3)
   └── Complete application forms
   └── Prepare supporting letters
   └── Organize document package
   └── Quality control check
   └── Client review and signature

4. Submission (Week 4)
   └── Submit to Ministry of Home Affairs
   └── Pay government fees
   └── Obtain receipt and reference number
   └── Provide client with tracking info

5. Processing and Follow-up (Weeks 5-7)
   └── Monitor application status
   └── Respond to queries from ministry
   └── Submit additional documents if requested
   └── Follow up on timeline

6. Collection and Delivery (Week 8)
   └── Collect approved work permit
   └── Verify details for accuracy
   └── Deliver to client
```

- Ministry of Health (medical clearance)
- Guyana Police Force (local clearance if applicable)

**Post-Approval Requirements:**
- Work permit must be stamped in passport
- Employer must report commencement of employment
- Renewal applications should begin 2-3 months before expiry

**Common Grounds for Approval:**
- Specialized skills not available locally
