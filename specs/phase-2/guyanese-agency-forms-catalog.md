## GRA Forms

**Agency**: Guyana Revenue Authority
**Website**: https://www.gra.gov.gy
**Online Portal**: https://eservices.gra.gov.gy
**Contact**: (592) 227-6060

### 1. Individual Income Tax Return (Form 1)

**Purpose**: Annual tax return for individuals (employees, self-employed)
**Deadline**: April 30 of following year
**Processing Time**: 4-8 weeks
**Fee**: None (filing), Late fee: $10,000 GYD per month

**Required Fields**:
- Personal Information: Name, Address, TIN, NIS Number
- Employment Income: Employer TIN, Gross Income, PAYE Deducted
- Self-Employment Income: Business Name, Gross Receipts, Expenses
- Other Income: Interest, Dividends, Rental Income
- Deductions: Mortgage Interest, Medical Expenses, Donations
- Tax Calculation: Taxable Income, Tax Payable, Credits

**Supporting Documents**:
- TIN Certificate
- NIS Card
- 7B Slips (from employers)
- Bank statements (for interest income)
- Receipts for deductions

**Related Services**: KAJ Tax Returns, KAJ Compliance Certificates

---

### 2. Corporate Income Tax Return

**Purpose**: Annual tax return for companies, corporations
**Deadline**: 4 months after financial year-end
**Processing Time**: 6-12 weeks
**Fee**: None (filing)

**Required Fields**:
- Company Information: Name, TIN, Registration Number, Year-End Date
- Financial Statements: Assets, Liabilities, Revenue, Expenses
- Tax Computation: Net Income, Adjustments, Tax Payable
- Withholding Tax: Dividends, Interest, Fees Paid
- Director Information: Names, TINs, Shareholdings

**Supporting Documents**:
- Audited Financial Statements
- Tax Computation Worksheet
- Withholding Tax Returns
- Incorporation Certificate

**Related Services**: KAJ Corporate Tax Returns, KAJ Audit Services

---

### 3. PAYE Monthly Return (Form 5)

**Purpose**: Monthly payroll tax withholding report
**Deadline**: 15th of following month
**Processing Time**: Immediate (online submission)
**Fee**: None (filing), Late fee: $500 GYD per day

**Required Fields**:
- Employer Information: Name, TIN, Period (Month/Year)
- Employee Details: Names, NIS Numbers, Gross Pay, Tax Deducted
- Totals: Total Gross Pay, Total PAYE Deducted
- Payment Details: Bank, Date, Amount

**Supporting Documents**:
- Payroll register
- Payment receipt (bank deposit slip)

**Related Services**: KAJ PAYE Services

---

### 4. VAT Return Form

**Purpose**: Monthly Value Added Tax (VAT) return
**Deadline**: 15th of following month (for registered businesses)
**Processing Time**: 2-4 weeks (refunds)
**Fee**: None (filing)

**Required Fields**:
- Business Information: Name, TIN, VAT Registration Number
- Sales: Taxable Sales, Zero-Rated Sales, Exempt Sales
- Purchases: Taxable Purchases, VAT on Purchases
- VAT Calculation: Output VAT, Input VAT, Net VAT Payable/Refundable

**Supporting Documents**:
- Sales invoices
- Purchase invoices
- VAT payment receipt

**Related Services**: KAJ Tax Returns

---

### 5. TIN Registration Application

**Purpose**: Apply for Taxpayer Identification Number
**Deadline**: N/A (apply before starting business/employment)
**Processing Time**: 3-5 business days
**Fee**: Free

**Required Fields**:
- Personal/Business Name
- Address
- Contact Information
- Identification (National ID or Passport)
- Type of Taxpayer: Individual, Self-Employed, Company

**Supporting Documents**:
- National ID or Passport (copy)
- Proof of Address (utility bill)
- Business Registration (for companies)

**Related Services**: GCMC Company Incorporation, KAJ Tax Services

---

### 6. Tax Compliance Certificate Request

**Purpose**: Certificate proving tax obligations are current
**Deadline**: N/A (request as needed)
**Processing Time**: 5-10 business days
**Fee**: $5,000 GYD
**Validity**: 3 months

**Required Fields**:
- TIN
- Reason for Request (Tender, Land Transfer, Work Permit, etc.)
- Period Required (typically last 3 years)

**Supporting Documents**:
- Copies of last 3 tax returns
- Proof of payment for outstanding taxes

**Related Services**: KAJ Compliance Certificates

---

### 7. Withholding Tax Return

**Purpose**: Report taxes withheld on payments to contractors, interest, dividends
**Deadline**: 15th of following month
**Processing Time**: 2-4 weeks
**Fee**: None

**Required Fields**:
- Payer Information: Name, TIN
- Payee Information: Names, TINs, Payment Amounts, Tax Withheld
- Type of Payment: Services, Interest, Dividends, Rent, etc.

**Related Services**: KAJ Tax Services

---

### 8. Property Tax Return

**Purpose**: Annual property tax assessment
**Deadline**: March 31
**Processing Time**: 4-6 weeks
**Fee**: Varies by property value

**Required Fields**:
- Property Information: Address, Lot Number, Area
- Owner Information: Name, TIN, Contact
- Property Type: Residential, Commercial, Industrial
- Property Value: Self-assessed or GRA-assessed

**Related Services**: GCMC Consulting (for disputes), KAJ Tax Services

## NIS Forms

**Agency**: National Insurance Scheme
**Website**: https://www.nis.org.gy
**Online Portal**: https://esched.nis.org.gy
**Contact**: (592) 227-6851

### 1. E1 - Employer Registration

**Purpose**: Register as employer with NIS
**Deadline**: Before hiring first employee
**Processing Time**: 5-10 business days
**Fee**: None

**Required Fields**:
- Business Name
- Business Address
- Business Registration Number
- TIN
- Type of Business
- Estimated Number of Employees
- Start Date

**Supporting Documents**:
- Business Registration Certificate
- TIN Certificate
- Incorporation Documents (for companies)
- Director's ID

**Related Services**: GCMC Company Incorporation, KAJ NIS Services

---

### 2. E2 - Employee Registration

**Purpose**: Register employees with NIS
**Deadline**: Within 7 days of employment start
**Processing Time**: 3-5 business days
**Fee**: None

**Required Fields**:
- Employee Name
- Date of Birth
- National ID or Passport Number
- Address
- Job Title
- Start Date
- Monthly Salary

**Supporting Documents**:
- National ID or Passport (copy)
- Birth Certificate (copy)

**Related Services**: KAJ NIS Services, KAJ Payroll Services

---

### 3. F200F2 - Monthly Contribution Schedule

**Purpose**: Report monthly NIS contributions for all employees
**Deadline**: 14th of following month
**Processing Time**: Immediate (online)
**Fee**: None (filing), Late fee: $500 GYD per day

**Contribution Rates**:
- Employee: 5.6%
- Employer: 8.4%
- Total: 14%
- Maximum Insurable Earnings: $280,000/month

**Required Fields**:
- Employer NIS Number
- Period (Month/Year)
- Employee Details: Names, NIS Numbers, Earnings, Contributions
- Totals: Total Earnings, Total Contributions

**Supporting Documents**:
- Payroll register
- Payment receipt

**Related Services**: KAJ NIS Services, KAJ PAYE Services

---

### 4. NIS Pension Application

**Purpose**: Apply for retirement pension
**Deadline**: N/A (apply upon reaching retirement age)
**Processing Time**: 6-12 weeks
**Fee**: None

**Eligibility**:
- Age 60+ (men), 55+ (women)
- Minimum 750 contributions

**Required Fields**:
- Personal Information
- NIS Number
- Employment History
- Bank Account Details (for direct deposit)

**Supporting Documents**:
- Birth Certificate
- National ID
- Bank statement (for account verification)
- Marriage Certificate (if applicable)

**Related Services**: KAJ NIS Services

---

### 5. Registration Card Application

**Purpose**: Apply for NIS registration card (for individuals)
**Deadline**: N/A
**Processing Time**: 2-4 weeks
**Fee**: $1,000 GYD

**Required Fields**:
- Full Name
- Date of Birth
- National ID or Passport Number
- Address
- Contact Information

**Supporting Documents**:
- Birth Certificate
- National ID or Passport
- Proof of Address
- Passport photo

**Related Services**: KAJ NIS Services

---

### 6. NIS Benefit Claim Form

**Purpose**: Claim NIS benefits (sickness, maternity, injury, funeral)
**Deadline**: Within specified period for each benefit type
**Processing Time**: 4-8 weeks
**Fee**: None

**Required Fields** (vary by benefit type):
- Personal Information
- NIS Number
- Type of Benefit
- Dates of Incapacity (for sickness/maternity)
- Medical Details (for injury)

**Supporting Documents**:
- Medical Certificate
- Employer Statement
- Birth Certificate (for maternity)
- Death Certificate (for funeral)

**Related Services**: KAJ NIS Services

## Immigration Forms

**Agency**: Ministry of Home Affairs - Immigration Department
**Website**: https://moha.gov.gy
**Contact**: (592) 226-1590

### 1. Work Permit Application

**Purpose**: Apply for work permit for foreign nationals
**Deadline**: Must apply BEFORE entering Guyana for employment
**Processing Time**: 4-8 weeks
**Approval Validity**: 6 months (reduced from 1 year in 2025)
**Fee**: $25,000 GYD application, $100,000+ GYD permit fee (varies by position)

**Required Fields**:
- Personal Information: Name, DOB, Nationality, Passport Number
- Employment Details: Employer Name, Position, Salary, Start Date
- Qualifications: Education, Experience, Skills
- Employer Information: Company Name, TIN, Registration, Contact

**Supporting Documents** (applicant):
- Valid passport (6+ months validity) - all pages
- Passport photos (4 copies)
- Police clearance certificate (apostilled, 6 months validity)
- Medical certificate (blood type, physical check, 6 months validity)
- Educational certificates
- CV/Resume

**Supporting Documents** (employer):
- Business registration
- TIN certificate
- Job offer letter
- Company profile
- Proof of salary (bank statement or employment contract)

**Related Services**: GCMC Immigration Services

---

### 2. Business Visa Application

**Purpose**: Short-term visa for business activities (meetings, conferences)
**Deadline**: Apply 2-4 weeks before travel
**Processing Time**: 1-2 weeks
**Validity**: Up to 3 months
**Fee**: $15,000 GYD

**Required Fields**:
- Personal Information
- Purpose of Visit
- Duration of Stay
- Accommodation Details
- Sponsor/Inviter Information (Guyanese company)

**Supporting Documents**:
- Valid passport (6+ months validity)
- Passport photos (2 copies)
- Business invitation letter
- Flight itinerary
- Hotel reservation
- Bank statement (proof of funds)

**Related Services**: GCMC Immigration Services

---

### 3. Citizenship Application

**Purpose**: Apply for Guyanese citizenship
**Deadline**: N/A
**Processing Time**: 12-18 months
**Fee**: $80,000 GYD (application), varies for processing

**Eligibility**:
- 7+ years continuous residence, or
- Marriage to Guyanese citizen (5+ years), or
- Guyanese descent

**Required Fields**:
- Personal Information
- Residency History
- Employment History
- Family Information
- Reason for Application

**Supporting Documents**:
- Birth certificate
- Valid passport
- Police clearance (home country + Guyana)
- Proof of residence (7 years)
- Marriage certificate (if applicable)
- Employment records
- Tax returns (5 years)
- NIS contributions record

**Related Services**: GCMC Immigration Services

---

### 4. Passport Renewal Form

**Purpose**: Renew Guyanese passport
**Deadline**: N/A (apply before expiry)
**Processing Time**: 2-4 weeks
**Fee**: $10,000 GYD (regular), $15,000 GYD (express)

**Required Fields**:
- Personal Information
- Current Passport Details
- Contact Information

**Supporting Documents**:
- Current passport
- Birth certificate
- National ID
- Passport photos (2)

**Related Services**: GCMC Immigration Services (for foreign nationals renewing)

---

### 5. Visa Extension Application

**Purpose**: Extend stay beyond initial visa period
**Deadline**: Before current visa expires
**Processing Time**: 1-2 weeks
**Fee**: $5,000 GYD

**Required Fields**:
- Current visa details
- Reason for extension
- Extended duration requested

**Supporting Documents**:
- Passport
- Current visa
- Proof of financial means
- Letter explaining reason for extension

**Related Services**: GCMC Immigration Services

## DCRA Forms

**Agency**: Deeds and Commercial Registries Authority
**Website**: https://dcra.gov.gy
**Email**: commercialregistry@dcra.gov.gy
**Contact**: (592) 226-7092

### 1. Business Name Registration

**Purpose**: Register a business name (sole proprietor or partnership)
**Deadline**: N/A
**Processing Time**: 3-5 business days
**Fee**: $6,000 GYD (processing), $2,500 GYD (annual renewal)
**Validity**: Renewable annually

**Required Fields**:
- Business Name (unique)
- Business Address
- Type of Business
- Owner/Partner Information: Names, Addresses, IDs
- Nature of Business Activities

**Supporting Documents**:
- National ID or Passport (owner/partners)
- Proof of Address (utility bill)

**Related Services**: GCMC Business Registration

**Important**: Business name registration does NOT provide personal liability protection. For liability protection, incorporate a company.

---

### 2. Company Incorporation Form

**Purpose**: Incorporate a limited liability company
**Deadline**: N/A
**Processing Time**: 7-14 business days
**Fee**: $25,000 GYD (incorporation), $10,000 GYD (annual return)

**Required Fields**:
- Company Name (unique, check availability first)
- Registered Office Address
- Type of Company: Private, Public, Non-profit
- Share Capital: Authorized, Issued, Par Value
- Directors: Names, Addresses, IDs (min 1, max unlimited)
- Shareholders: Names, Addresses, Share Allocations
- Company Secretary: Name, Address
- Objects of the Company: Business activities

**Supporting Documents**:
- Memorandum of Association
- Articles of Association
- Declaration of Compliance
- Directors' consent to act
- National ID or Passport (directors/shareholders)

**Related Services**: GCMC Company Incorporation

---

### 3. Business Renewal Form

**Purpose**: Annual renewal of business name registration
**Deadline**: Anniversary of registration
**Processing Time**: 1-2 business days
**Fee**: $2,500 GYD
**Consequence of Non-Renewal**: Business name removed from register

**Required Fields**:
- Business Registration Number
- Business Name
- Confirmation of no changes (or submit changes)

**Supporting Documents**:
- Current registration certificate
- If changes: supporting docs for changes

**Related Services**: GCMC Business Registration

---

### 4. Change of Company Details

**Purpose**: Update company information (directors, address, shares, etc.)
**Deadline**: Within 30 days of change (AML Amendment 2023)
**Processing Time**: 3-5 business days
**Fee**: $2,000 GYD per change

**Types of Changes**:
- Change of Directors (addition, removal, details)
- Change of Registered Office
- Change of Company Secretary
- Change of Beneficial Ownership
- Transfer of Shares
- Change of Company Name (additional $5,000 GYD)

**Required Fields** (vary by change type):
- Company Registration Number
- Description of Change
- Effective Date
- New Details

**Supporting Documents** (vary by change type):
- Board Resolution approving change
- Consent forms (for new directors/secretary)
- Share transfer instruments (for share changes)
- Proof of new address (for address changes)

**Related Services**: GCMC Business Registration, GCMC Consulting
