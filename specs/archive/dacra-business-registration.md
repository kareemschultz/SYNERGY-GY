## 1. BUSINESS NAME REGISTRATION

### Overview
A business name is a name or style under which any business is carried on, whether in a partnership or otherwise. It does not create a separate legal entity from the owner, meaning the owner remains personally liable for all business debts.

### Eligibility Requirements
- Must be at least 18 years of age
- Citizens and residents of Guyana with valid identification
- Foreign nationals (must comply with immigration and work permit rules)

### Required Documents
1. **Business Name Application Form** (available from Commercial Registry office or website)
2. **Copy of National ID or Passport**
3. **For Non-Residents:** Valid passport copy and registered Power of Attorney
4. **For Estate Business Trustees:** Trustee letter plus Form R.B.N 2a (per Section 4 of the Business Names Act Chapter 90:05)

### Fees
- **Processing Fee:** GYD 6,000
- **Annual Renewal Fee:** GYD 2,500
- **Statement of Change:** GYD 2,000

### Processing Time
**3 working days** per application

### Process
1. Conduct name search and reservation
2. Complete Business Name Application Form
3. Submit form with required documents to DCRA office (in person)
4. Pay registration fee
5. Receive certificate of registration within 3 business days

### Annual Requirements
- Registration must be renewed every year
- Renewal fee: GYD 2,500

### Change Notifications
When changes occur to registered office, principal place of business, or ownership, notify the Registrar within 1 month of the change.

## 2. COMPANY INCORPORATION

### Overview
Incorporating a company creates a separate legal entity that protects owners from personal liability. The company is governed by the Companies Act Chapter 89:01 and the 1991 Act of the Laws of Guyana.

### Eligibility Requirements
- Must be at least 18 years old
- Not bankrupt
- Of sound mind
- Minimum of 1 director (natural person or corporate entity, no residency requirement)
- Minimum of 1 shareholder (natural person or corporate entity, no residency requirement)
- Must have registered office address in Guyana

### Required Documents

1. **Name Search and Name Reservation** (Form 18)
2. **Articles of Incorporation** containing:
   - Proposed company name
   - Registered office address in Guyana
   - Share capital details (rights, privileges, restrictions, conditions, payment terms)
   - Size of board of directors

3. **Notice of Directors** (Form)
4. **Consent to Act as Director** (for each director)
5. **Notice of Secretary** (Form)
6. **Consent to Act as Secretary**
7. **Notice of Registered Office**
8. **Declaration of Compliance** - signed by directors and shareholders confirming all legal requirements met
9. **Statutory Declaration** by attorney-at-law on suitability of signatories
10. **National ID Card or Passport** (for all directors and shareholders)

### Fees
- **Incorporation Fee:** GYD 60,000 (filed at DCRA)
- **Prescribed Fee:** GYD 60,000
- **Notices:** GYD 3,200
- **Mandatory Copy:** GYD 350
- **Total:** Approximately GYD 63,900

### Processing Time
**3 working days** - Certificate of Incorporation issued upon completion

### Process
1. Conduct name search and reserve company name (Form 18)
2. Prepare Articles of Incorporation
3. Obtain consents from directors and secretary
4. Prepare Declaration of Compliance
5. Submit all documents to DCRA with fees
6. Receive Certificate of Incorporation within 3 business days

### External Companies
An external (foreign) company cannot legally carry on any undertaking in Guyana until it is registered under the Companies Act Chapter 89:01.

**Required Forms:**
- Certificate of Registration of External Company
- External Company – Application for Registration

## 3. ANNUAL RETURN FILING REQUIREMENTS

### Companies Act Requirements

**Section 153 of the Companies Act** requires that every company file an annual return at least once per year.

### Annual Return Details
- Must be in prescribed form
- Made up to the date of the Annual General Meeting (AGM)
- Contains particulars set out in the Fifth Schedule of the Companies Act
- Must be signed by a director or secretary of the company
- Must be accompanied by audited financial statements

### Filing Deadline
**Within 42 days of the AGM**

### Responsible Authority
- The Registrar of Companies (reports to Attorney General and Minister of Legal Affairs)
- AML Department at DCRA handles:
  - Annual Return submissions
  - Financial Statement submissions
  - Beneficial Ownership declarations
  - Notice of Defaults for non-submission
  - Voluntary and Involuntary Winding Up
  - Liquidation of Companies
  - Cessation of External Companies
  - Receivership and removal of companies from register
  - Restoration of companies

### Legal Framework
- **Local Companies:** Part I, II, and III of the Fourth Schedule, Division F, Companies Act of Guyana, 1991
- **External Companies:** Section 328 of the Companies Act of Guyana, 1991

### Penalties for Non-Compliance
Companies failing to file annual returns may be struck off from the register of companies (published in the Official Gazette).

## 4. BENEFICIAL OWNERSHIP REQUIREMENTS

### Legal Requirements
**AML/CFT Amendment Act No. 12 of 2023, Section 4**

Every body-corporate must:
- Provide accurate and up-to-date beneficial ownership information to its Registrar
- Notify Registrar within 30 days of any change in particulars

### Changes Requiring Notification (within 1 month)
- Change of registered office
- Change of principal place of business
- Change of beneficial ownership
- Change of director or partner

### Required Form
- **Beneficial Ownership Application Form** (available from DCRA)

## 5. NAME SEARCH AND RESERVATION PROCESS

### Purpose
To ensure the proposed business or company name is unique and not identical or similar to existing registered entities.

### Required Form
**Form 18** - Name Search and Name Reservation (under Companies Act of Guyana)

### Process
1. Download Form 18 from DCRA website or obtain from office
2. Submit completed form to DCRA
3. DCRA conducts search of existing names
4. If name is available, reservation is granted
5. Proceed with business name or company incorporation application

### Important Notes
- The proposed name must be unique
- Cannot be identical or too similar to existing registered names
- Name reservation is required before incorporation
- Third-party services available for name search assistance

## 7. POST-REGISTRATION REQUIREMENTS

### 1. Guyana Revenue Authority (GRA) Registration
**Purpose:** Obtain Tax Identification Number (TIN)

**Required For:**
- Tax filing and payments
- Income tax
- Value Added Tax (VAT)
- Corporation tax

**Fees:**
- TIN Registration: GYD 1,000

**Processing Time:**
- Can be completed in 1 day if documentation is in order

**Tax Rates (2025):**
- Sole Proprietors (taxed as individuals):
  - 25% on chargeable income up to GYD 3,120,000
  - 35% on income above GYD 3,120,000
- Corporation Tax: Due 30th April each year
- Quarterly advance payments required (15th of March, June, September, December)

**Penalties:**
- Failure to file by 30 April: Additional 10% charge on outstanding tax
- Late payment: 2% per month penalty plus 18% annual interest
- Late loss/deficit return: GYD 50,000 flat fee

### 2. Value Added Tax (VAT) Registration
**Deadline:** Within 15 days of business operations beginning

**Processing Time:**
- Certificate issued within 10 business days if application is in order

### 3. National Insurance Scheme (NIS) Registration
**Required For:**
- All employers
- Self-employed individuals

**Purpose:**
- Contributions toward social security benefits
- Coverage for sickness, maternity, old-age pensions

### 4. Record Keeping Requirements
**Requirement:** Companies must keep proper accounts and records

**Retention Period:** At least 8 years after completion of transactions, acts, or operations

## 8. KEY DISTINCTIONS

### Business Name vs. Company

| Aspect | Business Name | Company |
|--------|--------------|---------|
| Legal Entity | Not separate from owner | Separate legal entity |
| Liability | Owner personally liable for all debts | Limited liability protection |
| Personal Assets | At risk if business fails | Protected from business bankruptcy |
| Registration Fee | GYD 6,000 | GYD 60,000 |
| Processing Time | 3 days | 3 days |
| Complexity | Simple registration | More complex incorporation process |
| Annual Renewal | GYD 2,500 | Annual return + financial statements |

### Profit vs. Non-Profit Companies

**To Convert:** Articles of Incorporation must be amended following the Fourth Schedule of the Companies Act Chapter 89:01.
