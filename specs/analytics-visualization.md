# Analytics & Data Visualization

## 1. Dashboard Analytics

### Overview Stats

**Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)**
- **Total clients**
  - Current count with percentage change from previous period
  - Breakdown by business unit (GCMC/KAJ)
  - Active vs inactive ratio

- **Active matters**
  - Total count by status (open, in-progress, pending, closed)
  - Percentage change from previous period
  - Average matter duration

- **Pending deadlines**
  - Total upcoming deadlines
  - Urgent count (within 7 days)
  - Overdue count with severity indicators

- **Revenue this month**
  - Current month revenue
  - Comparison with previous month
  - Year-to-date total
  - Target vs actual

- **Completion rate**
  - Matters completed on time percentage
  - Deadline compliance percentage
  - Staff productivity score

### Charts

**Matters by status (donut chart)**
- Segments: Open, In Progress, Pending, Completed, On Hold
- Center displays total matter count
- Interactive hover showing count and percentage
- Click to filter dashboard by status

**Matters by business (pie chart)**
- GCMC vs KAJ distribution
- Revenue contribution per business
- Matter count per business
- Interactive drill-down to business details

**Revenue trend (line chart)**
- Monthly revenue over 12 months
- Comparison with previous year
- Projected revenue trend line
- Hover shows detailed breakdown

**Client growth (line chart)**
- New clients per month over 12 months
- Cumulative client count
- Client churn rate overlay
- Growth rate percentage

**Deadline completion rate (gauge chart)**
- Percentage of deadlines met on time
- Color-coded zones: Red (<70%), Yellow (70-85%), Green (>85%)
- Last 30/60/90 day selector

## 10. Visualization Components

### Chart Types

**Line Charts**
- Trends over time
- Multiple series comparison
- Area variation for cumulative trends
- Annotations for key events
- Zoom and pan capabilities

**Bar Charts**
- Comparisons across categories
- Horizontal and vertical orientation
- Grouped and stacked variants
- Sorted by value or category
- Interactive tooltips

**Pie/Donut Charts**
- Proportions and percentages
- Limit to 5-7 segments for clarity
- "Other" category for small segments
- Center label for total
- Exploded segments for emphasis

**Area Charts**
- Cumulative trends
- Stacked areas for composition
- Stream graphs for flow
- Confidence intervals

**Gauge Charts**
- Single metric visualization
- Target zones (red, yellow, green)
- Percentage or absolute values
- Trend indicator

**Heatmaps**
- Time patterns (hour Ã— day)
- Intensity by two dimensions
- Color gradients
- Interactive tooltips

**Scatter Plots**
- Correlations between two variables
- Bubble size for third dimension
- Trend lines
- Quadrant analysis

**Table Visualizations**
- Sortable columns
- Conditional formatting
- Inline sparklines
- Row expansion for details

### Interactive Features

**Hover Interactions**
- Detailed tooltips
- Context-specific information
- Value formatting
- Trend indicators

**Click Actions**
- Drill down to details
- Filter dashboard
- Navigate to related view
- Expand/collapse sections

**Date Range Selector**
- Quick presets (Today, This Week, This Month, etc.)

**Filter Persistence**

**Export Capabilities**
